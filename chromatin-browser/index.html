A minimal genome browser for two chromatin marks over a single locus: H3K27me3 (Polycomb-repressed, broad domains) and Pol II S5p (initiating polymerase, sharp promoter peaks). Move across the track to read signal; called peaks are marked beneath the ruler.

Signal is drawn from a seeded generator: H3K27me3 as low-frequency broad enrichment, Pol II S5p as Gaussian peaks at synthetic promoters. Peaks are called by a per-track threshold and merged across adjacent bins. Swap the generator for a real bedGraph to view your own tracks.
