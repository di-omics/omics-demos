chromatin-browser

A minimal genome browser for two chromatin marks over a single locus: H3K27me3 (Polycomb-repressed, broad domains) and Pol II S5p (initiating polymerase, sharp promoter peaks). Drag to zoom; click reset to return. Called peaks and gene models are shown beneath the ruler.

all signal is synthetic - generated in-browser, no data files

H3K27me3 Pol II S5p drag to zoom, hover for values →

Signal is drawn from a seeded generator: H3K27me3 as low-frequency broad enrichment, Pol II S5p as Gaussian peaks at synthetic promoters. Peaks are called by a per-track threshold and merged across adjacent bins. Drag horizontally to zoom into a region; click reset to return to the full view. Swap the generator for a real bedGraph to view your own tracks.
